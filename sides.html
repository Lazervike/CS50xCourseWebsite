Sides Recipes

I try to have my sides be classic yet novel. A lot of these recipes are foods people have definitely tried, but with a bit of twist it can bring up a new level of excitement for everyone.

The items in the "threes" changes year to year, but there's one that's consistent, the mashed potato. This is probably my favorite recipe to eat, its not easy to make per se, but the effort is definitely worth it.

Some may disagree with me for listing a lamb dish here. After all, meat dishes are traditionally viewed as mains. I want to challenge that notion here. The dish's flavor profile serves well to supplement, not drive, the presentation of the entire meal set
